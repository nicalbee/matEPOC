toolbox: matEPOC
date: 17-Aug-2015
Authors: Nic Badcock & Johnson Thie
contact: nicholas.badcock@mq.edu.au

explanation:
This is a MATLAB based toolbox for importing the Emotiv EPOC EEG TestBench created .edf files.
The major motivation for this toolbox is to all for the inspection and editing of physical event markers in the EEG signal.
This are created as per the descriptions in the following Open Access articles:
Badcock, N. A., Mousikou, P., Mahajan, Y., de Lissa, P., Thie, J., & McArthur, G. (2013). Validation of the Emotiv EPOCÂ® EEG gaming system for measuring research quality auditory ERPs. PeerJ, 1, e38. http://doi.org/10.7717/peerj.38
Badcock, N. A., Preece, K. A., de Wit, B., Glenn, K., Fieder, N., Thie, J., & McArthur, G. (2015). Validation of the Emotiv EPOC EEG system for research quality auditory event-related potentials in children. PeerJ, 3(e907). http://doi.org/10.7717/peerj.907

Please see matEPOC_example.m script for a demonstration of the toolbox.
